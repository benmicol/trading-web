What we value the most....

We are very agile and people oriented company. Everything we do is vastly user-centric. This make our users irreplaceable for the development and success of OpenTrade web site. As agile company, we believe in iterative development through incrementally delivering features gathered via constant feedbacks from our actively participating users and members of our community.


This process would be constantly evaluated and optimized with every iteration and delivering cycle. New features and governance issues would also be constantly reviewed and refined with increasing control of the community to ensure they remain effective and reflect the latest consensus and information available.


Finally, we envision great user satisfaction as well as strong commitment. We are passionate about our principles like respect other people, do the right thing, be socially responsible in everything we do (even business), be open-minded and accept mistakes, and last but not least have lots of fun and fulfillment doing all of that.
